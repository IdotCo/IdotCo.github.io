Snake Game

  • Eat the apple containing the answer for the equation
  • You get a point and a life when eating the correct apple
  • If you eat an incorrect apple you lose a life
  • If you collide with the wall or yourself you go back down to a maximum of three lives and gets to start in the middle
  • To move the snake use the arrow keys
